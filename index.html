About WiggleWise

WiggleWise is an engaging toddler flashcard app designed to make learning fun and interactive. With colorful images, clear pronunciation, and cheerful music, your little one will love exploring new words and concepts.

Key Features:

  • 300+ Flashcards across Animals, Fruits & Vegetables, and Objects
  • Clear Pronunciation with text-to-speech technology
  • Cheerful Music that makes learning enjoyable
  • Parental Controls with secure purchase verification
  • No Ads or Tracking - completely safe for children
  • Offline Learning - works without internet connection

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock all flashcards?

You can unlock all 100+ flashcards per category through an in-app purchase. The purchase is protected by a parental gate to ensure only adults can make purchases.

Is my child's data safe?

Absolutely! WiggleWise doesn't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All learning happens locally on your device.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Once downloaded, WiggleWise works completely offline. No internet connection is required for learning.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you've previously purchased the full version, tap the "Restore Purchase" button in the app to unlock all content.
